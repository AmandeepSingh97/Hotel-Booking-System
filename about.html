About This Hotel

Darling Residency is the No.1 hotel in Vellore providing a perfect distraction-free environment for both Business and Leisure travelers. Right from luxurious rooms, Multi-Cuisine Veg and Non-Veg Restaurants, spacious Banquet halls and elegant Bar, one can be assured of a comfortable stay

Come, experience simplicity at its best with the ingeniously designed Smart Space rooms. Our rooms are designed with comfort and convenience in mind; providing a place to unwind or to catch up on work. All the rooms have adequate ventilation with windows and also come with ceiling fans and air conditioned with individual temparature adjustment facility. Every room has contemporary and elegant setting, designed to ensure relaxed ambiance. All the standard hotel rooms and family rooms are designed to have lavish interiors and stunning comfort. All beautifully tastefully furnished rooms are provided with all the facilities and amenities like attached bathroom, hot water, TV and other requisite amenities.

Aaradhana A/C Veg Restaurant serves varieties of tasty and nutritious vegetarian dishes. We provide vegetable curries real hot 'n' spicy and so add a number of spices to make them really exotic. These are also prepared using different methods of cooking like baking, boiling, frying etc. We offer a number of tempting Indian vegetarian recipes of dishes like Navratan Korma, Shahi Paneer etc.
